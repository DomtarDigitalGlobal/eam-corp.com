The EAM advantage
from start to finish

Engineered Absorbent Materials (EAM Corporation) has been developing and manufacturing innovative, high quality absorbent core solutions for many of the world's largest and most successful branded and private label consumer product manufacturers since 1998.

Our headquarters and manufacturing center is conveniently located in Jesup, Georgia near Savannah, one of the busiest container ports in North America. The facility includes a state-of-the-art research complex, where our team of scientists and engineers are developing the next generations of absorbent materials for future consumer and industrial applications.

To learn more about our business, please refer to our Frequently Asked Questions.
