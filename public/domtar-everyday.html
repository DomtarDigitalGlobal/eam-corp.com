Proud to be part of Domtar Corporation
A global leader in pulp, paper and absorbent hygiene

EAM is a wholly owned subsidiary of Domtar Corporation. Domtar acquired EAM in 2012 as a foundational piece of its Personal Care division.

Domtar is a leading provider of a wide variety of fiber-based products including communication, specialty and packaging papers, market pulp and absorbent hygiene products. With approximately 10,000 employees serving more than 50 countries around the world, Domtar is driven by a commitment to turn sustainable wood fiber into useful products that people rely on every day. Domtar’s annual sales are approximately $5.1 billion and its common stock is traded on the New York and Toronto Stock Exchanges (NYSE: UFS, TSX: UFS). Domtar’s principal executive office is in Fort Mill, South Carolina.
