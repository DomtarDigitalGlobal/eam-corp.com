International Nonwovens Industry Associations

  • INDA, Association of the Nonwovens Fabrics Industry

    Based in Cary, North Carolina, INDA's mission is to promote the growth and profitability of the nonwovens/engineered fabrics industry.

    www.inda.org

  • EDANA

    Headquartered in Brussels, EDANA is an international association serving the nonwovens and related industries in Europe, Africa and the Middle East.

    www.edana.org

  • ANFA, Asia Nonwoven Fabrics Association

    ANFA represents the nonwoven industry in Asia (Japan, Korea, China, Taiwan, Hong Kong and other Asian countries).

    www.asianonwovens.org

  • TTNA, Technical Textile & Nonwovens Association

    The TTNA represents the interests of the technical textile and nonwovens industry in Australia.

    www.ttna.com.au
